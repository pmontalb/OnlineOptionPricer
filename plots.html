Analytic Formula
Price $$P_C = e^{-rT} \left(S e^{bT} \cdot N(d_1) - K \cdot N(d_2)\right)$$ $$P_P = e^{-rT} \left(K \cdot N(-d_2) - S e^{bT} \cdot N(-d_1)\right)$$
Delta $$\Delta_C = e^{(b - r)T} \cdot N(d_1)$$ $$\Delta_P = e^{(b - r)T} \cdot N(-d_1)$$
Gamma $$\Gamma_C = \Gamma_P = {e^{(b - r)T} \over S\sigma\sqrt{T} } \cdot P(d_1)$$
Vega $$V_C = V_P = e^{(b - r)T} S\sqrt{T} \cdot P(d_1)$$
Rho $$\rho_{C, P} = {\partial P_{C, P}\over \partial r} + {\partial P_{C, P}\over \partial b}$$ $$ {\partial P_{C, P}\over \partial r} = -T \cdot P_{C, P} $$
Carry Rho $$\rho^C_{C, P} = {\partial P_{C, P}\over \partial b}$$ $$ {\partial P_{C, P}\over \partial b} = S\cdot T \cdot \Delta_{C, P} $$
